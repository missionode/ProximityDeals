Help & FAQs

How referrals work

Proximity Deals is referral‑only. Ask an existing user to show their referral QR. Scan it on first launch to join.

Creating coupons

  • Offer: needs expiry and a max usage count.
  • Service: no expiry, unlimited uses.

Scanning & charges

Redeem by scanning a coupon’s QR (or paste payload for testing). You are charged ₹1 only after the first redemption of each local day (IST).

Proximity score

Your visibility grows with coupon usage and referrals. A gentle decay on idle days keeps feeds fresh.

Moderation

Three unique flags suspend a coupon. Creators can appeal once.

Backups

Use Preferences to export/import all data as JSON. Auto‑save runs locally every 15s.
